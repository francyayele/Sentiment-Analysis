VADER

VADER stands for Valence Aware Dictionary and sEntiment Reasoner. It is a lexicon and rule-based sentiment analysis tool designed to analyze sentiments expressed in text, particularly in the context of social media. VADER is specifically tuned to work well with short and informal texts commonly found in platforms like Twitter, Facebook, and other social media channels.
